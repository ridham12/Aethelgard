Collections

Three expressions of the Athelgard code.

Classic

Architectural dials, polished restraint.

For black-tie dinners, legacy desks, and rooms where understatement commands the most attention.

 Sporty

Tension, traction, and technical confidence.

Sharper bevels, stronger contrast, and a performance stance built for weekends that still require taste.

 Vintage

Patina-minded elegance with memory in the metal.

Softened numerals, warm detailing, and case profiles informed by the golden era of trackside timing.

Craftsmanship

Every surface is treated like it will be examined in silence.

From the brushed shoulders of the case to the proportion of the hands against the chapter ring, the work is deliberate and slow. The result feels closer to an heirloom commission than a purchase.
